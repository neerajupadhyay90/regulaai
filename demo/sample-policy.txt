# Sample Company Privacy & Data Policy
## TechStart India Pvt. Ltd. — Internal Policy Document v1.2

### 1. Introduction
TechStart India Pvt. Ltd. ("the Company") is a B2C SaaS platform serving customers in India, the European Union, and the United States. We operate an e-commerce analytics platform that processes data for retail businesses.

### 2. Data We Collect
We collect the following types of data from end users:
- Full name and email address
- Billing address and payment card details (last 4 digits only; full card processed by Stripe)
- Usage telemetry and behavioral analytics data
- IP address and device fingerprint
- Purchase history and transaction data

We also collect data from our business clients (B2B):
- Company name, address, and GST number
- Contact person details
- API usage logs

### 3. How We Use Data
User data is used for:
- Providing the analytics service
- Sending product update emails and marketing communications
- Improving our machine learning recommendation models
- Sharing aggregated, anonymized data with our advertising partners

### 4. Data Storage
All data is stored on AWS S3 (Mumbai region) and replicated to AWS S3 (Ireland region) for redundancy.
Our database is hosted on AWS RDS PostgreSQL.
We use Google Analytics and Mixpanel for behavioral tracking.
We use Intercom for customer support chat.

### 5. Data Retention
We retain user data for as long as the account is active. Upon account deletion, data may be retained for up to 3 years for legal and audit purposes.

### 6. Third Party Sharing
We share data with:
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Google Analytics (behavioral analytics)
- Mixpanel (product analytics)
- Intercom (customer support)
- AWS (infrastructure)
- Our advertising partners (anonymized behavioral data)

### 7. Security
We use HTTPS for all data in transit.
Passwords are hashed using bcrypt.
We conduct annual security reviews.
Employee access to production data is unrestricted by role.

### 8. User Rights
Users may contact support@techstart.in to request data deletion. We will respond within 60 days.

### 9. Cookies
We use functional, analytics, and advertising cookies. Users are shown a cookie banner on first visit.

### 10. Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance.
